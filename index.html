ABOUT US

Excellence in Chemical Distribution

Connecting global quality chemical resources with Chinese innovation

Your Trusted Partner in Fine Chemicals

We specialize in distributing premium fine chemicals from world-renowned manufacturers including DOW (USA), OXEA (Germany), INEOS (Germany), Matrica (Italy), SK (Korea), DAICEL (Japan), KLK OLEO (Malaysia), and Musim Mas (Indonesia).

With strategically located warehouses and port facilities in Ningbo, Shanghai, Nanjing, Qingdao, Tianjin, Dalian, and Guangzhou, we ensure rapid delivery and exceptional service to customers across China and beyond.

🎯 Our Mission: To be the bridge connecting global excellence in chemical innovation with end-users, fostering mutual success through partnership.

Our diverse client base spans pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, animal nutrition, personal care, liquid crystal intermediates, lubricants, coatings, food additives, fragrances, and electronic chemicals.
